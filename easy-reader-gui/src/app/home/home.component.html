What's that / Short Overview

Short project overview and description

Study English by reading what you really like. In original without using any vocabulary.

How this works

You load your favourite book or any text
Possible Formats: txt, doc, fb2
Easy Ready read your book
You have your words with translation and ready to study

Why you should use this

Automatic translator is designed for getting some fast result. The translation quality is quite poor and you don't learn anything at all. We are open source project and we want to provide a platform for everybody to study English language

Our Motivation:

Convenience

Save your time. Read what you like without looking at the vocabulary

Study

Read in the original language. «Skill up» your word pack and get used to expose your self into foreign language
