What's that / Short Overview

Short project overview and description

Study English by reading what you really like. In original without using any vocabulary.

How this works

You load your favourite book or any text
Possible Formats: txt, doc, fb2
Easy Ready read your book
You have your words with translation and ready to study

Why you should use this

Automatic translator is designed for getting some fast result. The translation quality is quite poor and you don't learn anything at all. We are open source project and we want to provide a platform for everybody to study English language

Our Motivation:

Convenience

Save your time. Read what you like without looking at the vocabulary

Efficiency

Your brain is always in the process of leaning language

Study

Read in the original language. «Skill up» your word pack and get used to expose your self into foreign language

How application understands what you know?

Based on your profile vocabulary. Each word has two states "New", "In progress", "Learned". Words that you know stored inside your private profile. Each read book increases your vocabulary bank

Application helps to learn words

How it's done:

1. The main thing — list of words. It's built for the whole text or just for a part. There is a common list of words for learning.

2. Word using statistic is build based on your texts. Learn the most often words first.

3. It's easy to switch from reading to words learning. Convenient for big texts. For instance - you've looked at the words of next 5 pages → read the text → repeat the words → continue with the next 5 pages

4. Print Words. Learn words on the way or in the spare time

5. Words export. Import words for studying in the programs like (Anki, Memrise and etc.)
