Step 1. Registration

Go to registration form (you don't need to verify your email, this step takes about 1 minute) After that - you're ready to kick of.

Step 2. Upload any English Text

For text upload click on «Add Book» button on the left menu

Step 3. Check unknown words

1. Go to your book list

2. Click on the book

3. Click on the word and check at as «Learned», or «In progress»

Step 4. Look at the options regarding words list

On the words list page you can do the following:

1. Filter your words by part of the speech, status and etc

2. Sort words (the order they follow on the book or quantity of using the text)

3. Show and hide the translation and context from the text

4. Print Words

5. Export words in Excel, PDF and Anki

Step 5. Export your word list and start learning

You can export your words in the Excel or PDF format. If you want to learn your words set via Anki, you should use option «Export in Anki»
